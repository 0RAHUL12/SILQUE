The Founders' Vision

Redefining Indian hospitality.

We want to redefine the luxury of the Indian dining experience by introducing Airlaid napkins, which have historically been absent in the Indian context.

A napkin is the single most intimate touchpoint on a dining table—the only element that remains in constant contact with a guest's skin throughout the meal. We believe that true luxury dining shouldn't force premium establishments to compromise between the unhygienic reuse of cloth laundry and the flimsy feel of ordinary paper. By utilizing advanced airlaid technology, we deliver a pristine, single-use material that drapes like linen, absorbs like cloth, and guarantees absolute touch-hygiene. Our vision is to elevate Indian table presentation to match the country's rapid culinary evolution, ensuring a considered, spotless guest experience from the very first touch.

The Comparison

Overcoming Traditional Drawbacks

Why modern luxury establishments are shifting from linen and basic tissue to airlaid.

Cloth Linen Drawbacks

  • ✕ High water, chemical, and laundry overheads.
  • ✕ Progressively fades, stains, and frays.
  • ✕ Hygiene concerns of repeated reuse.

Ordinary Paper Drawbacks

  • ✕ Thin, cheap appearance lowers guest perception.
  • ✕ Tears instantly; guests waste multiple tissues.
  • ✕ Leaves annoying white dust/lint on glasses.

SILQUE Airlaid Overcomes Both

  • ✓ Linen drape & texture: Premium weight.
  • ✓ 100% Lint-free: Spotless presentation.
  • ✓ Eco-efficient: Saves laundry resources.

Tactile Luxury

Premium Weight Heavy 60-75 GSM fluff pulp structure provides a thick feel that mimics fabric. Crisp Fold Retention Stiff enough to support complex decorative folds for banquets and events. Exceptional Softness Gentle on the skin, offering guests a luxurious, comfortable experience.

Conscious Dining

95% Water Saved Dry-laid production loops conserve thousands of liters compared to wet mills. 100% Biodegradable Sourced from sustainable wood pulp, returning naturally to the soil within weeks. Zero Laundry Footprint Eliminates the hot-water energy and chemical waste of linen laundry.
