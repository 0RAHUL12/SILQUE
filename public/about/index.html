About Us

About SILQUE

SILQUE is a Bengaluru-based airlaid napkin company focused on refined single-use table presentation for professional hospitality.

What We Do

Focused airlaid napkin supply for hospitality buyers.

SILQUE supplies premium airlaid napkin formats for hotels, restaurants, cafes, caterers, events, banquets, corporates, and distributors. Our range is focused on three professional service needs: 8x8 cocktail napkins, 16x16 dinner napkins, and pocket cutlery napkins.

We help buyers choose the right format based on service style, table presentation, quantity, colour requirement, delivery city, and sample requirement.
