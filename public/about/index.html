About SILQUE

Premium airlaid napkin formats crafted in Bengaluru for high-end B2B hospitality, banquets, and fine dining settings across India.

Our Vision

Redefining single-use table presentation.

"We founded SILQUE with a singular focus: to elevate the guest experience. By combining the luxury weight of linen with the hygienic convenience of single-use material, we help hospitality leaders run pristine services without operational overhead."
Why We Exist

Bridging the gap between luxury and utility.

Traditional woven linen requires heavy laundry operations, water consumption, sorting, pressing, and is prone to wear. Standard paper napkins, on the other hand, shred instantly and fail to match the grandeur of a fine dining setup.

SILQUE premium airlaid napkins provide the perfect B2B solution. Crafted from 60-75 GSM dry-laid fluff pulp, our napkins have a tactile linen-like texture, exceptional absorption speed, and hold crisp decorative folds. We help luxury hotels, caterers, and venues deliver consistent luxury at every cover.

Precision Manufacturing

Produced in state-of-the-art facilities using dry-laid air forming technology. We ensure uniform GSM thickness, texture, and strength across every batch.

Lint-Free Cleanliness
