How to choose the right format

  • Choose 8x8 cocktail napkins for drinks, counters, and compact service moments.
  • Choose 16x16 dinner napkins for full table settings and premium dining.
  • Choose pocket cutlery napkins for buffets, catering, and organised cutlery presentation.
  • Choose custom printed napkins for events, hotel branding, launches, and signature dining.
