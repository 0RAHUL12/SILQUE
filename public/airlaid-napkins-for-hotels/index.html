Procurement Context

Napkins should support the service workflow, not just the table.

Hotels and hospitality procurement teams need napkins that look premium, feel substantial, and perform consistently during service. SILQUE focuses on 60-75 GSM airlaid material because it gives hospitality buyers a better balance of softness, absorbency, table presence, and single-use hygiene than standard paper napkins.

For quotation, share the service format, expected quantity or monthly usage, colour preference, delivery city, and whether custom printing is required. Samples can be discussed before bulk ordering so the team can evaluate texture, fold, absorbency, and presentation.

FAQ

Which SILQUE napkins are recommended for hotels?

SILQUE usually recommends the format that matches the service moment: 40x40 dinner napkins for full table service, 20x20 cocktail napkins for beverages and counters, and pocket cutlery napkins for organised buffet or event service.

What GSM range does SILQUE supply?

SILQUE airlaid napkins are available in the premium 60-75 GSM range, with final selection confirmed by format and buyer requirement.
