Sample & Supply Planning

How hotels can source premium airlaid table napkins in India.

For hotels, restaurants, caterers, and banquet teams, napkins are part of the guest experience as well as the procurement workflow. SILQUE TISSUES supplies premium 60-75 GSM airlaid napkins for buyers who want a cloth-like feel, strong table presence, absorbency, and hygienic single-use convenience.

Bulk enquiries should include product format, monthly or event quantity, colour preference, delivery city, custom print requirement, and sample requirement. SILQUE can then guide the buyer toward the correct format: 8x8 cocktail napkins for beverages, 16x16 dinner napkins for full-service dining, pocket cutlery napkins for buffets, or custom printed airlaid napkins for branded hospitality experiences.
