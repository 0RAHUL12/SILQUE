The Sourcing Standard

Tailored branding with zero operational friction.

SILQUE custom printed airlaid napkins help premium hospitality venues establish visual consistency. Sourced from organic pulp and crafted with cloth-like weight, our custom printed solutions offer high-definition logo visibility without the expensive setup, laundry, and replacement cycles of cloth linens.

Custom Sourcing Terms

  • Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ): 10,000 napkins per custom print layout.
  • Production Lead Time: Ready in 7 to 10 days from layout approval.
  • Bangalore Delivery: Direct door-to-door delivery right to your restaurant, hotel, or banquet warehouse.

Branding & Fold Capabilities

  • Flexo Printing: High-precision logo printing using up to 2 colors with food-safe inks.
  • Metallic Hotfoil: Premium hotfoil printing options for high-end events and luxury dinners.
  • Embossing/Debossing: Elegant hot embossing or debossing textures for subtle, tactile logo impressions.
  • Standard Folding: 1/4 fold for 8x8 cocktail and 16x16 dinner formats; 1/8 book fold for pocket cutlery napkins.

Linen-Like Weight

Premium 60-75 GSM nonwoven cellulose has the exact look, drape, and texture of premium linen fabric.

Spotless Presentation

Our printing process ensures the inks do not bleed and the material remains 100% dust and lint-free on tables and dark clothing.

Direct B2B Sourcing

Transparent wholesale pricing, direct billing, door-to-door dispatch, and dedicated commercial account support.

Base Colour Options

Available in refined hospitality colours.

Standard base napkin colors include white, ivory, champagne, charcoal, navy, sage, burgundy, and blush. Custom print designs are matched against these bases using color-fast inks.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for custom printed napkins?

Our minimum order quantity (MOQ) for custom logo printing is 10,000 napkins. Standard unprinted stock is available at lower minimum tiers.

What is the delivery lead time for Bangalore venues?

Custom orders are processed, printed, and delivered door-to-door in Bangalore within 7 to 10 days from layout approval.

Can I request mockups or samples of custom folds?

Yes. Hospitality buyers can request physical sample packs or discuss logo positioning by sharing requirements over WhatsApp or email.
