Premium Airlaid Napkin Supply for Bangalore's Diverse Dining Scene

In a dynamic food capital like Bengaluru, table presentation is key to guest satisfaction. SILQUE manufactures high-quality airlaid napkins designed to meet the demands of local microbreweries, luxury business hotels, fine dining establishments, and high-volume catering services. Our airlaid cellulose material mimics the look and soft handfeel of heavy woven linen, but operates as a clean, single-use, hygienic disposable.

Because our corporate headquarters and warehousing are located directly in Bengaluru (Koramangala 6th Block), we can offer unmatched supply security and support. F&B managers can bypass standard shipping delays and access immediate stocks, quick local proofing, and local custom branding consultants.
