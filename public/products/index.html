How To Choose

Choose by service moment, not just by size.

Premium napkin procurement works best when each format is matched to a real service use case. A beverage counter needs a compact napkin with strong texture and absorbency. A full-service restaurant needs a larger dinner napkin that supports the table setting. A buffet or banquet operation needs fast, organised cutlery presentation.

SILQUE uses premium 60-75 GSM airlaid material, with the exact requirement confirmed by format, colour, print, and buyer use case. Product discussions can include colour preference, custom print requirement, delivery city, expected monthly usage, and sample requirement.

Colour Options

Choose colours for the service setting, not only the product size.

White and ivory suit fine dining and room service. Champagne, sage, navy, charcoal, burgundy, and blush can support event themes, banquets, bars, launches, and branded hospitality moments.

Colour availability, minimum quantity, and custom print suitability are confirmed during sample discussion.
