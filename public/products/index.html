The SILQUE Standard

What makes our supply different.

Every SILQUE napkin is manufactured from 100% virgin cellulose pulp using the dry-laid airlaid process — not recycled tissue, not imported commodity stock. The result is a material that feels like cloth, absorbs like linen, and arrives factory-fresh.

Virgin Cellulose, Not Recycled Stock

Every batch is produced from fresh, organic cellulose pulp — not recycled paper, not reprocessed tissue. The material is airlaid (dry-laid), giving it a consistent weight, texture, and softness that recycled or imported stock cannot replicate.

Bangalore Warehouse, Direct Delivery

Stock ships from our Bangalore warehouse directly to your venue — no middlemen, no forwarding delays. Standard orders dispatch in 7–10 days with door-to-door delivery across the city.

Your Brand on Every Napkin

Flexo printing, metallic hotfoil, or embossing and debossing — up to 2-colour ink on any format. Custom print MOQ starts at 10,000 napkins with layout approval to delivery in 7–10 days.

Clean, Dust-Free, Lint-Free

Airlaid cellulose is inherently clean — no dust, no lint, no odour. The material holds its integrity through service, leaving zero residue on hands, clothing, or dark surfaces. Safe for plated meals, wrapped cutlery, and guest-facing use.

Superior Absorbency, Zero Shedding

At 60–75 GSM, airlaid cellulose absorbs significantly more than standard paper napkins while leaving zero lint residue on hands, clothing, or dark surfaces. The material outperforms both tissue and imported economy stock.

Freshly Manufactured, Not Imported

Unlike commodity Chinese imports that sit in port warehouses for months, every SILQUE shipment is produced to order. Fresher material means better texture, consistent colour, and no storage degradation.
