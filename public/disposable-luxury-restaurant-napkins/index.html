What makes a disposable napkin feel luxury?

  • A visible texture that looks substantial on the table.
  • Softness that feels closer to fabric than standard paper napkins.
  • Absorbency that performs through the meal.
  • Clean folds and consistent shape during setup.
  • Optional custom printing for restaurants with a distinct brand experience.

FAQ

What are disposable luxury restaurant napkins?

They are premium single-use napkins designed to give a refined premium paper-like dining feel while maintaining hygiene and operational consistency.

Why choose airlaid napkins for restaurants?

Restaurants choose airlaid napkins because they are thicker, softer, and more absorbent than standard paper napkins and improve the perceived quality of the table setting.

Which SILQUE formats work for restaurants?

Restaurants commonly use 16x16 dinner napkins for table service, 8x8 cocktail napkins for drinks and counters, and pocket cutlery napkins for organised service.
