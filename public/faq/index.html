Why is airlaid considered water-efficient?

Airlaid is a dry-laid technology that distributes fibres with air rather than using water as the carrying medium in conventional wet-laid paper production. Industry sources report that premium airlaid napkins can use up to 95% less water during production than conventional paper napkins.
