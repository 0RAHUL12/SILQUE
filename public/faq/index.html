Sourcing & Delivery FAQs

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for custom printing?

For custom printed airlaid napkins, the minimum order quantity (MOQ) is 10,000 napkins per print layout. Standard, unprinted hospitality napkins are available in lower B2B wholesale tiers.

What is the lead time for custom printed orders in Bangalore?

Our typical production and printing lead time is 7 to 10 days from design layout approval to final delivery.

Do you offer door-to-door delivery within Bangalore?

Yes. We provide direct door-to-door B2B logistics to commercial venues, hotels, event spaces, warehouses, and banquets across Bangalore / Bengaluru.

What branding and custom printing methods do you support?

We support multiple custom branding options for hospitality logos:

  • Flexo Printing: Up to 2-color ink printing using safe, non-bleeding, food-grade inks.
  • Hotfoil Printing: High-end metallic hotfoil printing (ideal for luxury receptions and events).
  • Hot Embossing/Debossing: Textured logo presses directly onto the airlaid material.

What folding styles are available for each napkin format?

Our standard B2B folding formats are:

  • 8x8 Cocktail Napkins: 1/4 fold format.
  • 16x16 Dinner Napkins: 1/4 fold format.
  • Pocket Cutlery Napkins: 1/8 book fold format.

How can I request physical samples of the materials?

Hospitality procurement managers can request a free sample pack by contacting us over WhatsApp or emailing info@silquetissues.com. We provide samples in various base colors (white, ivory, navy, charcoal, champagne, etc.) so you can check the feel and weight in hand.

Why is airlaid paper considered a sustainable choice?

Airlaid is a dry-laid technology that distributes wood pulp fibers using air loops rather than water. This process uses up to 95% less water than conventional wet-laid paper tissue mills. Additionally, SILQUE wood pulp material is 100% biodegradable and compostable, returning naturally to the soil without laundry chemical waste.
