Table Presentation

Airlaid napkins make the table feel more premium.

A good napkin changes how the table is perceived before the guest even touches the food. Airlaid gives a fuller handfeel, cleaner fold, stronger table presence, and a more refined dining impression than ordinary thin paper napkins.

Colour also matters. White, ivory, champagne, charcoal, navy, sage, burgundy, and blush airlaid napkins can match the mood of a restaurant, event, banquet, or brand-led table setting.

Sustainability

Responsible single-use dining starts with better material choices.

Airlaid material is formed using a dry-laid process where fibres are distributed with air instead of using water as the carrying medium. That helps reduce process water demand compared with conventional wet-laid paper production.

For high-volume hospitality, the practical benefit is a premium single-use napkin that supports hygiene and presentation without requiring cloth laundering for every service moment.

SILQUE keeps sustainability claims grounded: the full carbon footprint depends on raw material sourcing, energy, logistics, usage, and disposal. Our focus is to supply a refined airlaid format that reduces avoidable waste in service, feels more substantial per use, and supports cleaner table operations.
