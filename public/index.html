Sustainability

Airlaid dry-laid technology.

Airlaid is a dry-laid technology: fibres are formed and compressed using air rather than water as the carrying medium. Because no large volume of process water has to be evaporated, industry references describe airlaid napkins as using significantly less water than conventional wet-laid paper napkins.

For high-volume hospitality, that matters: less production water, minimal wastewater, and a premium single-use format that supports hygiene without depending on cloth laundering for every service moment.
