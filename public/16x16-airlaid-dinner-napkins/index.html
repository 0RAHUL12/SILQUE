Service Fit

Built for full-service dining, banquet covers, plated meals, formal restaurant tables, hotel dining rooms, and premium catering.

SILQUE 16x16 airlaid dinner napkins are made for hospitality teams that want a premium single-use napkin without losing softness, absorbency, or table presence.
